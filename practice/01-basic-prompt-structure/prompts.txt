# Prompt
Can you tell me the color of the ocean?

# Claude's response
The ocean appears blue — though the exact shade varies depending on conditions:

- Deep open ocean: Deep, vivid blue
- Shallow coastal waters: Lighter blue, turquoise, or green
- Cloudy or stormy days: Grey or dark green
- Near river mouths or algae blooms: Brown or greenish

The blue color comes primarily from water molecules absorbing red wavelengths of light and scattering blue wavelengths back to our eyes — similar to why the sky is blue. Reflected sky color also plays a role.
